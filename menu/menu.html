How to Play

RBG = Red, Blue, and Green

Colors contain different amounts of red, blue, and green from 0 to 255.

For example:
Pure red: (255, 0, 0)
Pure blue: (0, 255, 0)
Pure green: (0, 0, 255)

You will be given a RBG number and you will will have to try to guess the correct color from a series of colored circles. You can start on easy mode (three color options) or jump right into hard (six color options).

Good luck!
